Like previously mentioned on other pages, I also played football and ran track in high school. Even today, I still enjoy going out and throwing a baseball or football around with friends and family. This has always been a way for me to destress when my life is getting hard. It also gives me chances to take in the beauty of the great outdoors. However, I am not just a fan of playing sports, I am also a fan of watching them. I was born a Chicago Cubs fan and became a Chicago Bears fan not too long after. By the time I was in my early teenage years, I also got into the sport of hockey. As you could probably figure out, I am a Chicago Blackhawks fan too. Sports have always been a huge part of my life. There is nothing more enjoyable to me than watching the Bears play on Sundays or watching the Cubs at Wrigley Field.
