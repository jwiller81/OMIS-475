Background

Where I Went to High School

Niles West High School is located in Skokie, Illinois and has been a high school institution since 1959.

High School Experience

Throughout high school, I was very active with sports. From freshmen year to senior year, I was involved in football and track. I was a student-athlete and proud of it. As well, in high school I was also very active in my classroom setting. By the end of high school, I had finished with a 3.0 GPA. It wasn’t the best, but I was always satisfied with how much work and effort I put in. Being an athlete at Niles West was one of the greatest accomplishments I have achieved in life so far. Many great athletes have been born at Niles West including: George Kontos, Rashard Mendenhall, and Jewell Loyd. Being apart of this school's traditions and expectations really made high school some of my best moments yet.

College Life

After high school ended, I was on my way to Northern Illinois University. By August 2015, I began my first semester at NIU. Nervous and ready to learn, I strived to be a better student in college than what I did in high school. I knew how important college was, and I didn’t want to be average at it. Through many hardships, I learned to be a better student, as well as a better person. With my focus on information systems and data analytics, I truly saw how right of a fit it was for me. I’m proud to say that I currently have a major GPA of a 3.5, along with my minor in data analytics. As well, I accomplished making Dean’s List in the Fall of 2018.
